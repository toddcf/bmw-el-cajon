BMW of El Cajon

I designed and coded these two landing pages for the 2019 BMW X7 and 8 Series. BMW is always a fun creative challenge because their marketing guidelines are very specific.

I am particularly proud of the button hover effects — something I'll point out or else they will certainly go unnoticed. I had to build these to BMW spec, but also managed to find a workaround to an unwanted "flashing" effect that results from CSS's inability to transition a linear gradient. To read a full description of this technique and view the code, click either of the GitHub links below.
